How Grades Affect Your CGPA

CGPA is calculated as a weighted average of grade points based on course credits. This means:

  • Higher-credit courses affect CGPA more
  • An S or A in a 4-credit course has strong impact
  • Projects and labs also carry credits

Important Notes

  • A U grade does not contribute to CGPA
  • Only passed courses are included in CGPA calculation
  • Retaken courses replace earlier attempts as per IITM rules
